About

Alex Kim!

I'm a web developer. I've immersed myself into a world of engineering, problem-solving, teamwork, and independent time management. Always wanting to build, I like to keep a number of projects going on that help me improve upon my skillset.

When it's time to take a break from coding, I like to remind myself that I am human with my other interests. I played as Principal Cellist in the Illinois Wesleyan Civic Orchestra, went on tour with my band, and later went on to be a bike mechanic at a local shop. I now have about 6 bikes. I like meeting fellow nerds that are also passionate about nerdy things.

melody

This was my first independent full-stack application in which I used node.JS, Express.JS, MongoDB, Handlebars, and bCrypt, to name a few technologies.
Melody is a place for musicians to find each other. It allows its users to browse through an archive of musicians' profiles and save others as favorites for possible contact.
No longer should you need to worry about finding other musicians in your area!

This can be classified as an Emergency Management app. It is meant to present important In-Case-of-Emergency (ICE) information. The app is meant to be used while camping, and contains the ability to hold information that is specific to the user's trip.

Ear Training

Just like classical ear training, the game presents the player with a melody. The player must listen closely to the melody and decipher which notes were played. A keyboard, built with JavaScript & AudioAPI, is available as either a tool to the player using clicks or the homerow of the keyboard.
